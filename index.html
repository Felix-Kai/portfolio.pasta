Creamy Garlic Shrimp Pasta

Ingredients

For the pasta

  • 8 oz (about 225g) of your favorite pasta (spaghetti, fettuccine, or linguine work well)
  • Salt, for pasta water

For the shrimp and sauce

  • 1 lb (450g) large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes (optional, for heat)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth (or white wine for more flavor)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)
  • Zest of 1 lemon (optional, for extra freshness)

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in salted water according to package instructions. Drain, reserving 1/2 cup of pasta water.
  2. Saute shrimp in olive oil and butter for 2-3 minutes until pink. Remove shrimp and set aside.
  3. . In the same skillet, cook minced garlic for 30 seconds. Add chicken broth (or wine), heavy cream, and Parmesan. Simmer for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add cooked pasta to the sauce, toss to coat. Return shrimp to the pan and mix.
  5. Garnish with parsley and lemon zest, then serve.
